IDEA

Single player, dodger, simple idea, the player controls 2 charaters at the same time, there is one map split in the middle, both characters are place on opposite sides, both controlled by WASD. the catch being that A for one charater is D and the same with WS, reflecting mechanics. there will be missles attacking both sides alternating each time. the goal is to survive as long as possible without either charators dying.
